Biography Duwayne "Tony" Smith, Pastor of Embrace Christian Church

Duwayne "Tony" Smith entered this world on August 16, 1959, to the union of the late Tucker Smith and Bertha Smith. From his parent's union, Pastor Tony is the second child of five, three brothers and one sister. Tony attended public schools in Hillsborough County, graduated June of 1977 from Brandon High School, Brandon, Florida. He continued his educational pursuit at Hillsborugh Community College (HCC) where he was a member of the baseball team from 1977 to 1979 and earned his Associate of Arts degree. Pastor Tony continued the studying of God's word at Liberty University, Tabernacle Bible College and Seminary wheree he earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in the Religious Art. Currently, he is working on earning his Master's degree and will not stop there!

During the summer of July 1987, Pastor Tony met his soul mate, Fayeshawn Devon Davis. On November 11, 1989, they were joined in holy matrimony. The proud parents of three beautiful children: Lauren Dione Smith White, Devin Anthony Smith, Destiny Alexis Lurline Smith and two grandsons.

He was introduced to God's word and spiritual teachings at an early age, Pastor Tony's first place of worship in God's house was Hosley Temple of Tampa, Florida. He continued his thirst for knowledge and understanding of God's word and spiritual teaching at Hosley Temple C.M.E. until the summer of 1990, Pastor Tony joined Allen Chapel A.M.E. Church Plant City, Florida, where his wife, Fayeshawn, had been a long-standing member. As his knowledge grew, he became very active in the church and served as a member of the Trustee board, Steward board, Allen Chapel mortgage committee, and Men of Allen spiritual group. In addition to serving in various capacities of leadership throughout the years, Pastor Tony was involved in a variety on ministerial endeavors that prepared him to embrace lost souls and minister to the whole individual regardless of challenges or limitations

Professional Journey:

Pastor Tony joined the Tampa Police Department in March of 1982 as a Police Officer. He experienced a very promising career in the Police Department, retiring as a Police Corporal/Detective in Jusly 2010. Also, he served as a youth mentor for Big Brothers/Big Sisters during his time as a Police Detective. Pastor Tony is currently employed with Hillsborough County Public Schools, assigned as a school security Corporal. He started the "Future of Tomorrow" mentoring group as Lithia Springs Elementary, Cimino Elementary and Alafia Elementary while assigned as a Community Service Officer.

Beginning of his spiritual calling and journey:

During the beginning of 2016, the spirit of the Lord came upon Tony and instructed him to start a church ministry, just as the Apostle Paul was assigned to do by God. By faith and the trust in God, Pastor Tony launched the Embrace Christian Church ministry.

His ultimate desire is to see people fully embrace God's love and to walk in victory and the power of our Lord while experiencing the fullness of God's promises. Pastor Tony is making a difference in the lives of people, members, and our community. Both, Pastor Tony, and his Wife, Fayeshawn, are following God's word and leading souls to Christ, they are blessed by the Holy Spirity in which they are a blessing to others.

Embrace Christian Church Vision:

It is our vision as a christian church minsitry to embrace the community based on God's vision and His words which are written in the book of life, which translates into the principles which we live by from the word of God. We are seeking to reach as many lost souls as possible based on our loving, kindness, compassion, giving, forgiving so that we all can grow together in one accord.

We are currently looking to establish a place of fellowship that we can call our own. We currently have our fellowship service at libraries and hotel conference rooms which at times we have to follow their schedule ir order to reserve space. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, we are currently using the zoom platform for our fellowship service.

We are also seeking to establish a christian learning environment for our school age students in the near future, which is much needed in today's society. Lets not just talk about it, let's be about it based on the plans set forth by God. Just as God had given Nehemiah the vision to rebuild the broken walls in Jerusalem (Nehemiah 2:17, 18 NIV), we too here at Embrace Christian Church seek to rebuild a broken world one soul at a time. God has placed on our hearts to help the homeless. Therefore, we are looking to provide food, clothing, shelter and basic needs for the homeless. We are currently helping charity organizations (see charity list) that help those in need.

Embrace Christian Church Mission:

First and foremost, we are all God's people. It is our mission here at Embrace Christian Church to teach God's people based on the written and spoken words of the bible. It is also our mission to encourage everyone to comprehensively teach the word of God. Our beliefs are in the power of God through the Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Our mission is to help those who stand in the needs of physical, emotional and spiritual needs. God has placed his people on the earth for their purpose in life so that God may be glorified. Therefore, it is our mission to assist all God's people to accomplish their purpose in life by the word of God. Join us in accomplishing our mission statement that God has placed on our hearts. We are a bible based ministry doing the will of God.
